Welcome to DrivingDecoded

Crack the Code. Pass the Test.

DrivingDecoded is designed to support learner drivers preparing for both their theory and practical driving tests.

It provides a simple, user-friendly space where learners can document how their lessons are going and reflect on their progress.

How DrivingDecoded Helps

Document Your Journey

Write down what you cover in each lesson, how you felt behind the wheel, and any challenges you faced. Keeping a driving journal helps you remember key moments and track your progress over time.

See Where You Can Improve

Use simple dropdowns in each entry to highlight areas you are working on, such as manoeuvres, confidence, or road awareness. Spot patterns and focus your practice where it matters most.

See How Far You Have Come

Look back at previous journal entries to see how much you have improved since your first lessons. Recognising your progress builds confidence and keeps you motivated.
